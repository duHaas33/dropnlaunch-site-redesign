INFORMATION WE COLLECT

Account Information: When signing up or logging in to the App, users are required to authenticate using Apple or Google credentials. This process is governed by the respective privacy policies of Apple or Google, and we disclaim any responsibility for their data collection practices. Upon successful authentication, users may optionally provide additional account details, including a name or nickname, birthdate, height, weight, and baseball or softball skill level.

The provision of these details is entirely at the user's discretion and is not mandatory for using the App unless a specific feature requires that information. Users are encouraged to use nicknames if they prefer to remain anonymous. The App does not verify the authenticity of the data provided, and users are solely responsible for the accuracy of their submissions.

Drop N Launch may also collect or store player profile information that you choose to provide, including first name, last name, player handle, sport, graduation year, country, state or province, city, batting stance, throwing arm, positions, teams and clubs, team links, social links, and other player profile information. Drop N Launch may support primary profiles and family player profiles. Family profiles may be managed by the account owner, parent, guardian, or other authorized user.

Performance Data: The App collects baseball and softball swing metrics during gameplay or training sessions. These metrics may include, but are not limited to, swing speed, launch angle, ball exit velocity, distance, and other performance-related data. Such data is collected exclusively for delivering personalized feedback, tracking progress, displaying stats, calculating rankings, and enhancing the user's experience within the App and related services.

Aggregated data, such as trends derived from swing metrics, age, height, and weight, may also be used for research, development, or marketing purposes. This aggregated data does not include personally identifiable information.

Device Information: To ensure optimal functionality and compatibility, the App may collect technical data related to the user's device, including but not limited to device type, operating system version, unique device identifiers, and diagnostic information. This data is used solely to troubleshoot technical issues, improve the App's performance, and maintain compatibility with supported devices.

Support and Reports: If you contact support or report a public profile, leaderboard issue, vulgar content, profanity, impersonation, inaccurate information, or another concern, we may collect your email address, message content, the page, profile, leaderboard, or issue being reported, and any other information you choose to include.

PUBLIC PROFILES, PLAYER HANDLES, AND LEADERBOARDS

Profiles are private by default.

A profile becomes public only when the account owner uses the public profile control in the App and confirms that they want to make the profile public. Links to this Privacy Policy and the User Agreement are shown near that control.

When a profile is private, Drop N Launch may still include the player in leaderboard calculations in a masked format. For example, a private profile may appear as "Private Profile" while showing limited ranking information such as age division, rank, and max exit velocity. Private profiles do not show a public handle, public profile page, name, city, state, country, teams, social links, or other identifying public profile details.

When a profile is public, Drop N Launch may publish a public player profile page and may show identifying profile information on public leaderboards. Public profile information may include player handle, display name, initials, sport, age division, graduation year, country, state or province, city, positions, batting and throwing information, selected performance stats, ranking information, teams and clubs, team links, and social or player profile links that the user chooses to provide.

Birthdate is treated as private profile information. We may use birthdate to calculate age division for profiles and leaderboards, but we do not display the player's full birthdate on public profile pages or public leaderboards.

If public profile visibility is turned off, the public profile page may become unavailable and leaderboard rows may return to a masked private format. Some cached pages, search results, screenshots, shared links, or previously viewed pages may take time to update or may remain outside Drop N Launch's control.

Users may choose a public player handle. Handles are used for public profile URLs and public identity in Drop N Launch. We may screen handles and public profile information for profanity, vulgar terms, impersonation, brand misuse, misleading content, or other prohibited content. We may reject, remove, hide, or change public visibility for content that violates our policies or creates a safety, privacy, or moderation concern.

Public visibility for family profiles should be controlled by the parent, guardian, or account owner. If you create, manage, or publish a family profile or a profile for a minor, you represent that you are the parent, legal guardian, or otherwise have permission to manage and publish that player's profile information.
